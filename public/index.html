Our Model

Group-Based Lending

We believe in the power of numbers, and the network effects of keeping a good thing going. Most of our clients are already in social groups, called Chama, where they practice table banking. Our business is an add-on.

Automated Support

We believe in the power of technology to do good. Our clients do not need a smartphone to interact with us or order new products. Their payments are automatically recorded and a payment receipt generated and sent to them as an SMS.

Dynamic & Sustainable

Our products are shaped by the feedback from our clients, enabling us to provide market-ready services that keep our clients coming back. Each client in a group can purchase a product of their choice, enabling them to truly be independent while still accountable to their group.

Our Impact

SDG 3: Good Health and Well-being

Our products like pressure cookers and clean cookstoves reduce household air pollution and improve cooking efficiency, contributing to better health and well-being.

SDG 7: Affordable and Clean Energy

By promoting the use of energy-efficient products like clean cookstoves and pressure cookers, we help reduce energy consumption and support sustainable energy use.

SDG 12: Responsible Consumption and Production

Our durable and eco-friendly products promote sustainable consumption patterns, reducing waste and encouraging responsible production.

Secondary Influences

Our activities also influences multiple SDGs: affordable goods and group-based lending lift people out of poverty (SDG 1) and reduce economic inequalities (SDG 10), efficient household products support gender equality (SDG 5), and promote sustainable living (SDG 11), while innovative and efficient appliances drive industry growth (SDG 9) and mitigate climate change (SDG 13), ultimately fostering decent work and economic growth (SDG 8).
